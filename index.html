About the Institution

Bhairahawa Multiple Campus originally named as Bhairahawa Bahumukhi Campus is affiliated under Tribhuvan University, the central university of Nepal. The college is located in Siddharthanagar area of Bhairahawa city. It runs as a non-profit educational institution in the city. Being a multiple campus, it runs various programs under science, management and humanities stream. Moreover, it runs +2 programs as well as Masters and Bachelors degree programs. Bachelors Degree Programs at Bhairahawa Multiple Campus are BSc, BBS and BA, Masters Degree Programs at Bhairahawa Multiple Campus are MBS, MAsters in Nepali and Masters in Sociology and Anthropology.
